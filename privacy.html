Capyture Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-10 · Back to home

English

Capyture is a browser extension that records browser tabs. It is designed to work entirely on your device.

Data we collect: none

Capyture has no servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising. The developer never sees your recordings, your settings, or any information about you.

Where your data lives

Recordings are written to temporary storage inside your browser profile during capture and then saved to your computer's Downloads folder. Settings (theme, language, recording options, meeting URL keywords, Drive folder links) are stored locally via the browser's extension storage. Nothing is transmitted to the developer or any third party.

Microphone and tab audio

Audio is captured only while you record or while the popup shows a live level preview, and is processed entirely on your device.

Google Drive upload (optional, off by default)

If you enable Drive upload and connect your Google account, recordings are sent directly from your browser to Google's Drive API over HTTPS using the minimal drive.file scope, which only permits access to files the extension itself creates. Your OAuth Client ID is encrypted (AES-GCM) and stored only on your device. The developer never receives your files, tokens, or identity. Google's handling of your data is governed by Google's own privacy policy.

繁體中文

Capyture 是一款分頁錄影瀏覽器擴充功能，設計上完全在你的裝置上運作。

我們收集的資料：無

沒有伺服器、沒有分析、沒有追蹤、沒有廣告。開發者不會看到你的錄影、設定或任何個人資訊。

資料存放位置

錄影過程中資料寫入瀏覽器設定檔內的暫存空間，完成後存到電腦的「下載」資料夾。設定（主題、語言、錄影選項、會議網址關鍵字、Drive 資料夾連結）儲存在瀏覽器的擴充功能本機儲存空間。任何資料都不會傳送給開發者或第三方。

麥克風與分頁音訊

僅在錄影或面板顯示即時音量預覽時擷取，全程在本機處理。

Google Drive 上傳（可選，預設關閉）

啟用並連結 Google 帳號後，錄影會由你的瀏覽器透過 HTTPS 直接傳送至 Google Drive API，使用最小權限 drive.file（僅能存取本擴充功能自己建立的檔案）。你的 OAuth Client ID 以 AES-GCM 加密後僅存於本機。開發者不會收到你的檔案、token 或身分資訊。

政策變更與聯絡方式

任何修改將在生效前發佈於本頁。問題請至 github.com/deadline314/Capyture/issues 開 issue。
